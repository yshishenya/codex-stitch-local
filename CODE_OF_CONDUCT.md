# Code of Conduct

This project follows a simple standard:

- be respectful
- be direct
- assume good intent
- criticize ideas and implementations, not people
- do not share secrets, private data, or unsafe content

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, abuse, or personal attacks
- discrimination or hateful conduct
- doxxing or sharing private information
- spam, low-effort trolling, or intentionally disruptive behavior
- malicious code, supply-chain abuse, or attempts to exfiltrate secrets

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block contributors when behavior harms the project or its community.

## Reporting

Open a private security report for vulnerabilities. For conduct issues, contact the maintainer through GitHub.
